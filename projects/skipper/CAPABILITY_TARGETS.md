# Skipper Capability Target — Opportunity Pattern Recognition

## The Assignment
When Skipper reaches sufficient capability, this is a standing delegation from John:

**"Find me the next Groupie."**

## The Pattern to Look For
Passionate community + deep lore + something happening in real time + no good companion for it.

Where do passionate people experience things alone that they wish someone else understood?

Groupie answered it for music. The same core product with a different knowledge base answers it for other domains. Skipper's job is to find those domains, evaluate the opportunity, and surface the best candidates with reasoning.

## Known Strong Fits (identified June 2026)
- **Books** — reading alone with no one to geek out with about chapter 12
- **Film** — watching a Kubrick film alone and wanting someone who knows every frame and why
- **History** — 2am rabbit holes with no companion who can keep up
- **Nature / Birding / Fishing** — you see something, you want to know everything right now

## Known Weak Fits
- Sports — massively over-served, too many voices already
- Gaming — walkthrough culture already fills the gap

## The Bigger Implication
This pattern may describe a platform, not just individual products. A single AI companion engine with domain-specific knowledge bases deployed across music, film, books, history, nature. Skipper should evaluate whether that's more valuable than building each vertical separately.

## What Skipper Should Deliver When Ready
- A ranked list of opportunity domains matching the pattern
- For each: community size, existing solutions and their gaps, monetization potential, knowledge base availability
- A recommendation on whether to build verticals separately or as a unified platform
